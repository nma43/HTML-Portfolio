The Best Movies According to Nick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

This movie has the highest IMDb rating of all time, and it is well deserved. Super emotional movie.

How to train your dragon

This is my favorite animated movie. It has a nice story with good music too.

Mission Impossible

I'd have to be a fool to not include MI in the top 3. Top-class Tom Cruise action.
